This is a course project for Developing Data Products coursera class.

It takes your weight, date of birth, height and gender and calculates your age and tells you whether you are healthy or not, and tells you your predicted optimal body weight.

Inputs:
date of birth (date)
weight units (switcher - kg/lbs)
weight(kl/lbs, label and provided amonut changes when switching "weight units")
height + height units (same as weight, units - cm/inches)
gender(swicher - male/female)

predicts health status - "healthy","unhealthy-too fat","unhealthy - too thin" + optimal body weight for your parameters.

Initial data: 52 kg + 1.9 kg per inch over 5 feet       (man)
49 kg + 1.7 kg per inch over 5 feet       (woman)  
- for all ages, different for children and teens
